1. Purpose

The initial hazard analysis (IHA) documents the preliminary hazard analysis for the device. The IHA is an essential activity to control/reduce residual risk from intolerable to acceptable based upon previously defined risk tolerance levels and forms part of the ONAVITAL risk management file.

2. Scope

The IHA is constructed in alignment with and applies the good practice principles of ISO14971:2012/2019.

3. Initial Hazard Analysis  

The following hazards correlate to the non-exhaustive list of hazards in Annex C of ISO 14971:2012.  Assess if the hazard listed is applicable to the medical device under evaluation.

All hazards identified as ‘YES’ (indicated through use of green highlight) must be transferred onto the FMEA Report (found here: OV E5.3. Failure Mode Effect & Analysis) and evaluated to determine whether the benefit outweighs the risk, when using the device.
