34

Product is not user friendly and/or difficult to use for final users

Firmware

The user must know when the device is starting.

Cold start-up of the system shall be indicated to the user by a steady red LED light.

With the battery fully discharged or no battery connected, connect the device to the external power supply and check that the red LED lights up for less than 2 seconds and then the LED turns blue.

DEV-823 - Obteniendo datos de incidencia... ESTADO

35

Product is not user friendly and/or difficult to use for final users

Firmware

User is able to know when the device have detected its presence.

Completion of the user presence detection process with a positive result shall be indicated to the user by a steady green LED light with a duration of 5 seconds.

With the device connected to the external power supply, disconnect the external power supply and put on the device. Next, check that the LEDs flash blue for a period of less than 30 seconds, followed by the LED turning green for a period of 5 seconds before turning off.

Pulse rate measurements must be comprehensive.

The pulse rate shall be indicated in beats per minute
