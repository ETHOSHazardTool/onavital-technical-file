4. Risk Analysis:

  • Phase 2 clinical investigations are ongoing in a hospital setting and will generate additional evidence to validate clinical benefits. However the results from prior Phase 1 testing and a review of literature and state of the art is summarised in the clinical evaluation report and provides sufficient data to conclude the device is safe for the intended uses.

  • Safety evaluation testing is in progress. Testing to date indicates a satisfactory output and we are awaiting a final report.

  • External cybersecurity testing has been scoped and testing is scheduled to complete in March.
