Purpose

Electromagnetic and Electrical Safety Testing of ONAVITAL. Tests according to the IEC 60601-1-11:2015/A1:2020 standard in conjunction with the IEC 60601-1:2005/A1:2012/A2:2020 standard.

Scope

General requirements for basic safety and essential performance - Collateral Standard: Electromagnetic disturbances - Requirements and tests (Endorsed by Asociación Española de Normalización in May of 2021.), as stablished by the standard UNE-EN 60601-1-2:2015/A1:2021.

Radioelectric disturbance emission tests according to EN 60601-1-2:2015+A1:2021.

Immunity tests to electrostatic discharges according to EN 61000 - 4 - 2.

Immunity tests to electromagnetic fields according to EN 61000 - 4 - 3.
