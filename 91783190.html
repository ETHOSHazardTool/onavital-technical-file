1. Purpose

The failure mode effects analysis (FMEA) documents the failure analysis for the device. The FMEA is an essential activity to control/reduce residual risk from intolerable to acceptable based upon previously defined risk tolerance levels and forms part of the ONAVITAL risk management file.

2. Scope

The FMEA is constructed in alignment with and applies the good practice principles of ISO14971:2012/2019.
