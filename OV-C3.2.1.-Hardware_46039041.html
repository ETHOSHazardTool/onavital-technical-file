4. Expected Life Time

Hardware Service life, and thus, Product Service life is determined by the Lithium Polymer battery Expected Cycle Life of 500 Cycles.

To avoid any risks, and considering an expected charge frequency of a full charge every 3 days, Service life is considered to be 4 years.
