1. Purpose

TO ensure ocmpliance with periodic safety update reporting requirements of the MDR.

2. Template

3. Responsibilities

The Person Responsible for Regulatory Compliance (PRRC)of Onalabs Inno-Hub SL, is responsible for the periodic safety update reporting for ONAVITAL.5. Data Collection

4. Frequency

A PSUR will be produced at least annually from the date ONAVITAL is placed on the European market inline with the MDCG guidance for class 11b non-implantable devices.

5. Exceptions

There are no exceptions to this plan except by senior management sign off . 

6. Enforcement

Onalabs Inno-Hub SL will perform regular audits to ensure policy compliance in accordance with the Internal Audit Standard Operating Procedure. Non-compliance will be escalated to business management.
