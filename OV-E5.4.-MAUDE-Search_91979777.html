1. Purpose

This record documents the results of MAUDE searches for associated medical device. The MAUDE search demonstrates activity to control/reduce residual risk from intolerable to acceptable based upon previously defined risk tolerance levels and forms part of the ONAVITAL risk management file.

2. Scope

The FDA MAUDE database's main utility is to identify potential problems associated with medical devices for further evaluation and research.

3. MAUDE Search Parameters

Problem reported between 31/03/2013 and 30/6/2023 or most recent 500 events for the following product classes:

870.2700 Oximeter, DQA

870.1130 Noninvasive blood pressure measurement system, DXN

880.2910 Clinical electronic thermometer, FLL

870.2910 Radiofrequency physiological signal transmitter and receiver, DRG
