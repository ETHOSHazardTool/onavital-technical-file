3.2 Packaging procedure

The packaging steps are the following:

 1. Open the container box.

  1. Pick the cable container from the box.

  1. Pick one cable and place this element inside the container.

  1. Place the cable container inside the box.

  2. Pick one ONAVITAL device, look at the UDI number on the back of the device and find the packaging label sticker that matches the UDI. Check for the serial number (SN on the product housing and GS1 – 21) and the GTIN (ID on the product housing and GS1 – 01 on the labelling).

  1. Place the device inside the container box.

  1. Put a user manual into the container box.

  1. Close the container box.

  2. Place the packaging label sticker on the side of the container box.

  1. Assemble the sleeve and slide it inside the container box.
